sample_model.ifc

IFC models are easily manageable with this library, in this case several available tools are developed for IFC files without optimizing, that is, as they are obtained of the original software. This is the beginning and the possibilities are endless if this is adds optimization procedures and data storage in more efficient formats.

sample_model.gltf

Interaction with GLTF extension models is also possible. The format has been noted as an important standard for augmented reality, integrating with modeling software such as Autodesk Maya, Autodesk 3ds Max, and Poly, best known for supporting 3D point cloud models and its increasing use in the AEC industry.
